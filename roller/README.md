# Roller
This is about as simple as it gets.  It's a simple dice roller, which allows me to generate  one or more dice rolls of a single type quickly

## Usage
```
./roller <dice type> <number of dice>
```

Example:
```
./roller 10 10
```

Output:
```
Your dice roll: 4
Your dice roll: 2
Your dice roll: 8
Your dice roll: 3
Your dice roll: 2
Your dice roll: 2
Your dice roll: 3
Your dice roll: 9
Your dice roll: 8
Your dice roll: 10
```
